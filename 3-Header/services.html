About this page: Here we add a complex header and explore 3 ways of handling its customisation on child pages.

Try:

  1. Open services.html and take a look at different degrees of how headers can be edited on child pages.
  2. Choosing the right approach depends on how flexible you need your header to be.
  3. Header 2 has optional areas. Optional areas are ok as long as you don't have too many of them.
  4. Header 3 is the most flexible. The whole thing is editable and we use components to make building headers on child pages easier.
  5. We could put any HTML into Header 3 because the whole area is editable. But that would be hard to maintain on multiple pages. So, instead we use components. If we needed to change the title we would just change the Header title component and update.
