About this page: This is a simple master page with two editable areas. On content pages that use this master page, the inner content of the two editable areas can be freely edited.

Try:

  1. Take a look at "Set as master page" that is added to the top element of the page. That makes this page a master page.
  2. Check how editable areas are defined: select an editable Column and check the Editable areas in ACT panel.
  3. Create new content page based on this master page: in PRJ panel, right click on Project name and select Add new page. Then choose a master page template from the list. On the new content page, edit the content of both editable areas. Do "Components -> Update" and see that the edits are preserved.
  4. Change the Header of Footer of the master page. Do "Components -> Update" and see that the changes are propagated to all content pages, while edits made in editable areas are preserved.
